I am working with [Prof. Frank Dellaert](https://dellaert.github.io/) as a contributor to the open-source Structure-from-Motion framework [GTSfM](https://github.com/borglab/gtsfm). I integrated 3D Gaussian Splatting optimization into the pipeline for dense reconstruction leveraging Dask for distributed parallel computing. More recently, I have been exploring 3D foundational models to generate 3D point clouds and Gaussians to enable large-scale reconstruction with novel clustering and merging algorithms.

I am also a Graduate Research Assistant at the [SocWeb Lab](https://socweb.cc.gatech.edu/) working with [Prof. Munmun De Choudhury](http://www.munmund.net/index.html) at the intersection of ML and mental health, evaluating modern ML methods, especially LLMs and their effect on mental health. My work has led to two papers:
- [Lived Experience Not Found](https://aclanthology.org/2025.naacl-long.553.pdf): Proposed a new methodology and benchmark to assess LLM performance in clinical decision-making for psychiatric treatment and harm reduction. The work was accepted as a Main Conference paper at NAACL 2025.
- [Reasoning is Not All You Need](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2505.20201?): Created a diverse dataset with clinically grounded synthetic multi-turn mental-health conversations with the conversation flow inspired by prior psychological literature. The work was accepted as a Main Conference paper at ACL 2026.

Some previous projects and works:
- Robust Saliency Prediction: Worked with [Prof. Matthias Bethge](https://bethgelab.org/) at the Tübingen AI Center and developed a robust saliency prediction model by incoporating Vision-Language Models (VLMs) surpassing state-of-the-art on fixation prediction benchmarks and improved generalizability. The work was accepted as a **[Highlight paper](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2025/papers/Kummerer_Modeling_Saliency_Dataset_Bias_ICCV_2025_paper.pdf) at ICCV 2025.**
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) based Agentic AI system: Built an end-to-end RAG based Agentic AI chatbot leveraging 7 years of proprietary conversation in the ethanol trading space and productionalized the application by deploying it on Azure using Dockers and Kubernetes to ensure modularity, security and scalability.  
- [Unlearning in LLMs](https://github.com/hkhanuja/Unlearning): Fine-tuned Alpaca 7B model to reduce bias in its responses using reinforcement learning inspired DPO loss variant.
- [DCC](https://github.com/hkhanuja/Detecting-Colorectal-Cancer): Trained a Computer Aided Diagnosis system to segment colon cancer from Whole Slide Images using CNN-Vision transfomer hybrid.
-  [NeuroNet](https://github.com/hkhanuja/Neuro_Net): Developed a custom model archictecure to classify patients with neurodegenerative from videos using a feature fusion from 3D CNNs and handcrafted features.
